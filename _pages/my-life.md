I am someone who really loves life outside research. Sports and time outdoors help me reset, stay disciplined, and keep a healthy sense of rhythm between work and everyday joy.

I am especially crazy about basketball, strength training, running, hiking, and climbing mountains. These experiences shape how I think and live: with energy, persistence, curiosity, and appreciation for progress that happens one step at a time.

Some sparks from my life:

- Basketball games that bring teamwork, rhythm, and instant happiness.
- Gym sessions that make consistency visible and rewarding.
- Runs that help me untangle ideas when research gets dense.
- Hiking trips and mountain walks that make the world feel larger and calmer.
- Ordinary student-life moments that become unforgettable because of the people, the place, and the timing.
